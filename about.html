About Me

Hello Visitor!

I’m Gianmarco, a passionate PhD student specializing in Robotics and AI.

I loved my journey through the world of screws, sudo rights, and bugs (way more than the one I'd like to admit) because this is one of my greatests passions.

During my PhD I met incredible people and got the chance to visit amazing places: London, the Netherlands, Japan, California.
Each one of those people and places made me the person I am today, gaving me support, inspiration, satisfaction, and a lot of nice food!

However, no human can thrive walking just one path.
That's why I usually walk in a zig-zag of other interests: music, poetry and literature, crafting. I really struggle walking straight.

As Einstein once said "For knowledge is limited, whereas imagination embraces the entire world, stimulating progress, giving birth to evolution."

This is how this website is born, a place for me to share all the crazy and less-crazy things my mind come up with.

In this website you will find poems, stories, thoughts that I wrote, together with information about crazy things I build and crafted, and with the nicest pieces of code I use my hands on.

One of the main reason behind why I decided to put all of it together is because we often forget that people are not straight lines, they do not have just 1-dimension.

We all are weird and complex shapes, difficult to understand and to appreciate from every angle. But it would be a shame for us to just show one of our dimensions, we are a combination of them, in every moment.

So, this is my way to really embrace who I am, quirks and all. If you don't do that, you're just hiding your unique spark.

Welcome to my world,
where science meets creativity, but who knows what is born from the union of them...
